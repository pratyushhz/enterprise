About Us

Established in the year 2016, Tiwari Enterprises in AT Road, Guwahati is a top player in the Guwahati for the scope of trading hand tools, garden tools, power tools, welding equipments, safety equipments, link chains and padlocks. This well-known establishment acts as a one-stop destination servicing customers both local and from other parts of Guwahati.

Our Products

Hand Tools

Categories of hand tools include wrenches, pliers, cutters, files, striking tools, struck or hammered tools, screwdrivers, vises etc.

Garden Tools

Categories of garden tools include the hatchet, axe, sickle, scythe, pitchfork, spade, shovel, trowel, hoe, fork, and rake.

Power Tools

A power tool is a tool that is actuated by an additional power source and mechanism other than the solely manual labor used with hand tools.

Welding Equipments

Welding is a fabrication process that joins materials, usually metals or thermoplastics, by using high heat to melt the parts together and allowing them to cool, causing fusion.

Safety Equipments

Safety Equipment or Personal protective equipment (PPE) is protective clothing, or helmets designed to protect the wearer's body from injury.

Link Chains

A chain is a serial assembly of connected pieces, called links, typically made of metal.

Padlocks

Padlocks are portable locks with a shackle that may be passed through an opening (such as a chain link, or hasp staple) to prevent use, theft, vandalism or harm.
